Traveling by air can be anxiety-inducing, and getting to or from Kempegowda International Airport (BIAL) is often a major hurdle for wheelchair users in Bangalore. Standard cabs rarely accommodate non-folding electric wheelchairs, and finding a reliable option is hard.

Our **Airport Wheelchair Taxi Service** ensures your journey starts or ends smoothly with absolute peace of mind.

## Dedicated BIAL Pickup and Drop Services

We recognize that airport travel requires punctuality, extra luggage space, and seamless boarding.

### What We Offer for Airport Travel

- **Hydraulic Lift Access**: No manual lifting. You simply roll onto the lift, and the machinery handles the rest.
- **Spacious Interiors**: Our modified vehicles easily accommodate the wheelchair passenger, plus 2-3 accompanying family members and luggage.
- **Trained Drivers**: Our chauffeurs help with loading and unloading luggage and properly securing the wheelchair before the long drive along Airport Road.

## How to Book Your Airport Transfer

To ensure availability, **we strongly recommend booking your airport ride at least 24 hours in advance**.

1. **Share Flight Details**: Let us know your flight number and scheduled arrival/departure time.
2. **Pickup Location**: Tell us if you are traveling from Whitefield, Jayanagar, Electronic City, or any other part of Bangalore.
3. **Luggage Estimate**: Give us an idea of how much luggage you'll have so we can ensure ample space.

## Arrival at BIAL: The Pickup Process

When you land at Kempegowda International Airport, navigating the arrivals area can be crowded. 

Our driver will be waiting at a designated arrivals bay for easy access. They will communicate with you directly via phone or WhatsApp, assisting you from the arrival terminal exit to the vehicle boarding point.

We track your flight, so even if there are delays, we adjust our schedule so your driver is ready when you are.

Say goodbye to the stress of standard airport cabs. Choose a ride structured around your accessibility needs!
